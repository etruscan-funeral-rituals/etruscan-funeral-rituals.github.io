Cinerary Urn (no lid)

Oct 5, 2021

Etruscan Cinerary Urn Inscription

Description

Here is another depiction of the popular five-figure battle scene associated with Chiusine workshops during the Hellenistic period.

Provenance and Period

It is from a town called Chiusi in Etruria, and it dates back to the 3rd or 2nd century BCE.

Inscription

Inscribed on the urn in red painted lettering across the top of it is:ARNTH HELE HERINIAL
Translation: Arnth Hele, son of Herinia.
