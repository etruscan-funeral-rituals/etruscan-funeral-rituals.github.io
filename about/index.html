Etruscan Funeral Rituals

This website explores some insight into the Etruscan burial practice. It explores some tombs in the Monterozzi Necropolis which is located in Tarquinia. Then it will display some cinerary urns from Chiusi which is a town located in Etruria. By exploring tombs and the art on the urns, you can get an inside look into the Etruscan funeral practices. The frescoes can shoe dipictions of the rituals that would take place.
